Land and building of an apartment unit (with the special status of unencumbered), registered as subplot No. 25824 subordinated from main lot No. 149, 1st lot partitioned from subplot No. 3498 of the mentioned main lot, located in Shemiran Region, Tehran City, Tehran Province, with an area of 213.95 Sqm. located on the north of 1st Flr., from which an area of 3.24 Sqm. is balcony. The property is inclusive of parking lot No. 1 with an area of 11 Sqm., located on the ground floor, and proportionate right from the land and other common parts of the premises in compliance with Apartment Ownership Act and its relevant executive directives.

Boundaries of the Property

North: in 3 sections, 1st) 4.36 m wall and window to the adjacent thoroughfare by the width of 20 m; 2nd) 2.3 m balcony’s balustrade to the adjacent thoroughfare by the width of 20 m; 3rd) 3.19 m wall and window to the adjacent thoroughfare by the width of 20 m. east: 10.42 m wall to the separation gap with subplot No. 2. West: 9.56 m wall to the separation gap with subplot No. 284. Ceiling and floor are jointly owned respectively with 2nd and ground floors.

Boundaries of the Parking Lot No. 1

North: 5 m arbitrary border to common area. East: 2.2 m arbitrary border to common area. South: 5 m arbitrary border to parking lot No. 2. West: 2.2 m arbitrary border to common area.  Ceiling is jointly owned with ground floor and floor is on common land of the property.
