Court Verdict of Custody

Concerning the petition of Ms. Nastaran Abdollahei, daughter of Hassan, in response to the request for custody of her nephew, Mr. SAMAN ABDOLLAHEI, 12-year-old, in light of the petition submitted, medical documents, photocopies of his birth certificate, and the contents of the case, including the plaintiff's statements, it is clear to the court that custody of Mr. SAMAN ABDOLLAHEI will be granted to the plaintiff forever.

The court considers the matter to be an instance of Article 1174 of the Civil Code and issues and announces a revised report in accordance with the agreement of the parties, based on the minutes of the session dated May 12, 2025, in accordance to Articles 184 and 187 of the Civil Procedure Code, in this way, the plaintiff assumes custody. The revised report issued is effective and valid for the parties and their legal representatives and is enforced in a timely manner, like court rulings.
