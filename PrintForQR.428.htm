Conditions of Divorce, Contracts and Divorce Contract Conditions

The lawyers of the couple arrived at the registry office with the necessary document to request the registration of their divorce.

Despite the attempts of the registry office to reconcile Zat al-Bin, the divorce was ultimately registered as requested.

According to the wife's lawyer's confession, the condition of the validity of the divorce is present and the wife is pure. According to the mentioned marriage document, the wife forbade all her rights and rights regarding the divorce

and the husband also accepted it. The couple has a child and the custody and alimony of the common child is the responsibility of the wife, and the right of visitation is reserved for the husband, considering that the woman has

ignored her dowry for the divorce and the husband has also agreed to it, it is a type of irrevocable Khula divorce. According to certificate No. 622327 dated 10/01/1824 and statement of the wife’s lawyer, the wife is not pregnant.

According to the decree number 140201290008402847, dated 22/12/1823, issued by branch 276 of Tehran Family Court No. 2, and with the full consent of the spouses, in the presence of the judges, and in compliance with the legal

and Sharia regulations, the divorce contract was issued and registered. It is explained to the wife that she should wait for purity for three periods.
