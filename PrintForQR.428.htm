1.      The lessee is not authorized to make use of the leasehold for any purpose rather than residence.

4.      Repair costs and general charges including installing and operating costs and expenses of ventilation, chauffage, cooler, lift and water, electricity and gas shall be paid by the lessor and minor costs and expenses related to the current use of the leasehold shall be paid by the lessee and the nature and amount of such expenses shall be determined in compliance with common law.

5.      Tenements taxes, major repairs charges, municipality charges and leasehold taxes shall be paid by the lessor, municipality business charges, income taxes (commercial and administrative) in favor of Taxation Department, and social security premium of workers shall be paid by the lessee.

7.      The lessee is bound to deliver the leasehold to the lessor against receipt in the same manner the lessor delivered to lessee and in case of any damages incurred to the leasehold, the lessee is liable to compensate.

8.      In case the lessee fails to vacate the leasehold on due time and / or for whatsoever reason refuses to vacate the leasehold, shall be bound to pay to the lessor a daily amount of 4,000,000 Rials as pecuniary indemnity of illegal possession of the leasehold after the expiration of the lease contract and trading off this amount with the cash deposit is permitted. 9.      Upon expiration of lease contract, the lessor is bound to take delivery of the leasehold and refund the cash deposit to the lessee; failing this the lessor is bound to pay to the lessee a daily amount of 4,000,000 Rials as pecuniary indemnity. Furthermore, payment of this pecuniary indemnity shall not contradict with judicial prosecution of the lessee for collection of the cash deposit.

11.  In other cases this contract shall be subject to Civil Code and Landlord & Tenant Act passed in 1997.

12.  This contract shall be merely valid if bear the seal and signature of real estate agency.
